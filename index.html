Popular Solutions

If you notice unauthorized transactions, follow these steps:

  1. Immediately call our fraud department at 1-800-432-1000
  2. Freeze your card through online banking or mobile app
  3. Submit a dispute form with transaction details
  4. Our team will investigate and typically resolve within 10 business days

To request a new debit card:

  1. Log in to your online banking account
  2. Navigate to "Account Services" then "Order New Card"
  3. Verify your shipping address
  4. Choose standard (7-10 business days) or expedited shipping (2-3 business days)
  5. Your new card will arrive with activation instructions

Transactions may be blocked due to:

  • Suspected fraudulent activity
  • Travel without notification (if used in new location)
  • Exceeding daily spending limits
  • Insufficient funds or account holds
  • Expired card information

Call our support line or use live chat to resolve this issue quickly.
